This data set has 400 observations on the following 11 variables.  Each observation records information about a single store and the region in which it is located.  The task is to predict the number of car seats (Sales) given information about the store and its region.

Sales:
Unit sales (in thousands) at this store

CompPrice:
Price in dollars charged by competitor in the region

Income:
Mean income level in the region(in thousands of dollars)

Advertising:
Local advertising budget for company in the region (in thousands of dollars)

Population:
Population size (in thousands)of the region 

Price
Price the company charges for car seats at this store

ShelveLoc
The shelving location of the car seats at the store.  Possible values are Bottom, Middle, and Top

Age
Average age of the population in the region

Education
Average education level in the region, measured in number of years

Urban
A factor with levels No and Yes to indicate whether the store is in an urban or rural location

US
A factor with levels No and Yes to indicate whether the store is in the US or not
